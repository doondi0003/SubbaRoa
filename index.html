About SRS Fitness

Hyderabad's trusted hands behind every workout machine.

Founded by Subba Rao, SRS Fitness Equipment Services has spent over a decade and a half restoring and maintaining fitness equipment for homes, hotels, residential complexes and full-scale commercial gyms across Hyderabad.

Whether it's a worn-out treadmill belt, a glitching massage chair, stiff multi-gym cables, or broken exercise bikes — our technicians arrive with original spares, the right tools, and decades of collective expertise.
